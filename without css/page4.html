Clues

Manuscript left behind:

Shortly before his murder, the victim mentioned that he was writing a novel about a true crime. Parts of this unfinished manuscript were found in his study, which seems to suggest that he knew some secrets that others did not want exposed.

Encrypted diary:

The victim has the habit of keeping a diary, but the contents of the diary are encrypted with a complex password. When the code is cracked, it reveals the victim's distrust of certain people and some disturbing premonitions.

An unsent letter:

An unsent envelope was found in the victim's desk drawer. The letter inside was written to an anonymous friend and discussed the victim's feeling that his life was threatened and mentioned several things. A suspect's name.
