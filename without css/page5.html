Final Reveal

Manuscripts, encrypted diaries and an unsent letter left behind by the victim become the key to unraveling the mystery. The manuscript reveals the victim's in-depth investigation into a historical crime that has a direct connection to Elena.

The encrypted diary was cracked and revealed the victim's suspicions about Elena and some of his investigations into her past. The unsent letter implicitly points to Elena and the past she is trying to hide.

Ultimately, as detectives uncover Elena's true identity and motives, a story of deceit, secrets, and past crimes unfolds. Elena realizes that once the victim's new work is released, her past will be nowhere to be found, and her achievements in the literary world and social status will be lost. In desperation, she chooses to silence her victims in order to protect her own secrets.

This case not only reveals the dark side behind literature, but also allows us to see the complex and multifaceted nature of human nature. In this story, the truth finally emerges, but it also leaves us with a profound reflection: How will people choose when fame and secrets are intertwined?

Now, with the mystery solved, the literary festival mystery has finally come to an end. But what it leaves to people is not only mourning for the tragic fate of a great writer, but also profound thinking about the real stories hidden behind the glamor.
